What is InteBot?

InteBot is a little project I threw together when attempting to make an IRC bot. I converted it over to a Twitch Bot when I realised that it could be better utilised on Twitch Channels than sat in a single IRC channel waiting for users.


Is InteBot free?

If you are using InteBot in a channel such as RizeUpGaming, RizeUpGamingTV or RevenantGamer, then you won't have to pay anything to use InteBot. If you wish to have InteBot in your own channel, there is a fee which is used to cover server fees and upgrades.

Can I be an Administrator/Developer of InteBot

Bot Admins are hand picked from people I trust. The Developer role is only ever for me.


What are the fees associated with having InteBot in my channel?

Because InteBot is ever growing, it will soon be put on a better server that can be upgraded easier. However this isn't free. The Price Plans for Intebot are as follows:

  • For $1.65, InteBot will remain in your channel for a Week <7 Days>
  • For $5.00, InteBot will remain in your channel for a Month <30 days>
  • For $50.00, InteBot will be hardcoded to remain in your channel until discontinuation.

Before you divulge, there is one thing to keep in mind. Chargeback will result in you being unable to use InteBot. Both in your own channel, and any channel it exists in. For all intents and purposes, InteBot will cease to acknowledge your existence.
